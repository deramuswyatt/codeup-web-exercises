What what the legend called before it was Noccalula Falls?

Does your park rent wheelchairs or strollers?
No, we do not.
I want to hike down underneath the waterfall. Is that allowed?
Yes, we have trails on each side of the park that lead down to and behind the falls.
Can pets ride the train?
Not unless they are ADA recognized Service Animals. No other animals are allowed (not counting Train Drivers of course)
Are campers allowed to have campfires?
Yes, campfires are welcome but only with a heavy dose of common sense.
Can we bring our own firepit?
Yes you can. Just make sure you do so safely.
Are there any primitive campsites in the campground.
No, no primitive sites are available.
